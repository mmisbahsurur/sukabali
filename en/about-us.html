About Us

Delve deeper into Suka Bali Agrotourism, born from a love for the diversity of nature and the agricultural richness of Bali.

Suka Bali Agrotourism is one of the agrotourism attractions located in Bali, precisely on Jalan Tirta, Manukaya Village, Tampaksiring District, Gianyar Regency. It boasts a strategic location surrounded by tourist attractions frequently visited by both local and international tourists, situated near Tirta Empul Temple and Gunung Kawi. Suka Bali Agrotourism officially opened on June 4, 2023.

History

The brief history of the development of Suka Bali Agrotourism began with the idea of creating a natural tourist destination that not only provides entertainment for visitors but also serves as a form of nature conservation, support for local farmers, and job creation. With determination and enthusiasm, the founders decided to develop this agrotourism area. They planted various types of organic crops, revived local agricultural wisdom, and created a variety of educational and recreational activities.

This agrotourism destination is named "Suka Bali" with the aim of inspiring and inviting people to love and appreciate the beauty of the island of Bali. The name "Suka Bali" was chosen with the hope that this destination would become a place that embodies the distinctive agricultural or plantation atmosphere of the island of Bali, allowing people to enjoy the island's beauty at this agrotourism location.
