Planation & Coffee Tour

Explore deeper into farming activities and the world of coffee by joining the Plantation & Coffee Tour activity.

Suka Bali Agrotourism offers an exciting travel experience during your holiday in Bali, where you can taste our local coffee, tea, chocolate, and civet coffee. Local Balinese farmers are highly creative in cultivating various types of coffee, but the two most commonly grown coffee species on this island are Robusta and Arabica.

Activity Details

Planation & Coffee Tour

  • Accompanied by a local tour guide
  • You'll be introduced to the Civet Coffee Making Process
  • Explore Suka Bali Coffee Plantation
  • Discover various spice plants.
This tour will take approximately 1 hour.

Coffee Making

Showcasing the coffee-making process that begins with: :

  • Introduction to the types of coffee beans
  • The coffee bean drying process
  • Post-Harvest Processing
  • The process of roasting coffee beans using traditional methods
This tour will take approximately 20 minutes

Coffee & Tea Tasting

Suka Bali provides a free tester menu, which consists of:

  • 1 Set Coffee tasting (7 cups)
  • 1 Set Tea tasting (7 cups)
  • Snack

Suka Bali Agrotourism proudly presents the authentic flavor of Bali Coffee, ensuring that every sip leaves an unforgettable impression.
